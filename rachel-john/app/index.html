John D. Rachel, MD, FACS

Quadruple Board Certified Surgeon

Thinking About the Whole Picture

Dr. John Rachel brings his artistry, expertise, and experience to the practice of facial and cosmetic surgery. He is quadruple board certified in Otolaryngology-Head and Neck Surgery, Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery, General Cosmetic Surgery, and Facial Cosmetic Surgery. Dr. Rachel does not dilute his focus; he has spent his entire career focused on cosmetic and reconstructive surgery of the face and body. He develops an individualized treatment plan for each of his patients and prides himself on providing them with the most natural looking results whether it is a Rhinoplasty, Face Lift, Liposuction, or any other cosmetic procedure. With his own boutique practice in Chicago and Glenview IL, and a state-of the art AAAHC-accredited facility, he is the surgeon people trust with their face and body.

Shaping The Future of Cosmetic Surgery

Thanks to his talent, advanced training and personalized approach to patient care, Dr. Rachel is a leading Chicagoland facial & body cosmetic surgeon. He has earned the appreciation and admiration of countless patients and colleagues. Dr. Rachel is an innovator and is actively involved in the advancements of the most current cosmetic technology and procedures. He continues to write publications and is intimately involved in the training and the qualifying process of future physicians. He is an active member of several prestigious medical organizations and has served on boards and committees for the American Medical Association, The American Academy of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery and The American Board of Cosmetic Surgery.
