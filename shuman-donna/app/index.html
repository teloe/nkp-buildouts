FAQ

What services do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of aesthetic services designed to help you look and feel your best at every age. Our services include: BOTOX® injections, IPL photo facials, laser hair removal, microblading, permanent makeup, chemical peels, microneedling, skin tag removal, cherry angiomas removal, dermaplaning, vein removal, electrolysis, bio-identical hormones, nutrient injections, and JUVÉDERM.

Do you offer a free consultation for aesthetic services?

Yes, we offer a complimentary consultation to help determine your treatment goals as well as which service(s) will help you achieve your desired results for your appearance and/or wellbeing.

Are laser treatments expensive?

Our services are competitively priced and very affordable. We offer monthly specials on services such as laser hair removal, photofacials, dermaplaning, and electrolysis to help you achieve your rejuvenated appearance as economically as possible.

Are laser treatments and other services safe?

We only offer safe and effective laser treatment, BOTOX® injections, and other services that have been approved by the Food and Drug Administration (FDA). Certain clients, including women who are pregnant, should check with their doctor before receiving elective treatments.

Are treatments painful?

None of our treatments are considered painful, though you may experience slight discomfort depending on the laser treatment(s) or aesthetic service(s) you choose. Many clients describe laser hair removal, spider vein treatment, and other laser therapies as feeling like a rubber band being snapped on the skin.

How many treatment sessions will I need?

Depending on the laser treatment you choose, you may need more than one visit to accomplish your treatment goal. Our technicians will estimate the number of sessions you will need during your initial consultation and stay in communication with you as treatments progress.

Can I see pictures of actual clients who have had the same treatment I’m considering?

View our before and after photo gallery to view the dramatic results from our clients who have undergone microblading, chemical peels, electrolysis, and other cosmetic services.

What are your business hours?

Our regular business hours are Monday through Friday, 9 am to 8:30 pm. We offer extended hours by appointment only, as well as emergency treatment to meet all our clients’ needs.

What should I expect during my initial visit?

During your complimentary consultation, one of our skilled laser technicians will listen to your skin care concerns. We then develop a personalized treatment plan to address each of those issues. Each of your treatment options will be discussed in detail to ensure you understand the procedures. The number of sessions will be determined, as well as pricing so you can make an informed decision about your care. In most cases, you may begin treatment on the same day as your consultation.

Do you accept insurance?

Most of our services are considered elective procedures and are therefore not covered by insurance. However, you may qualify for reimbursement by your insurance company in certain cases. Please contact your insurance company, as we do not handle any claims.
