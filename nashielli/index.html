Meet the doctor

Nashielli Torres Espinosa Chiu, MD

Doctor Nashielli Torres Espinosa Chiu is certified by the Mexican Board of Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery (CMCPER) (cert. number 1457). Dr. Nashielli studied medicine at the Universidad Autónoma de Guadalajara, with a specialty of General Surgery from the University Hospital Dr. Angel Leaño. Completed her education by doing the sub specialty of Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery at the Jalisco Institute of Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery under the direction of Dr. Jose Guerrero Santos, renowned for its extensive experience in the field of surgery, as a pioneer and founding professional.

International

Patients

We offer an extraordinary service for our patients coming to visit us from Canada and the USA or any country outside of Mexico! She will send any written information needed and will set up the next step, which is a video call in order to meet Dr. Nashielli and go over patients' detailed questions about procedures.
