Meet the doctor

Melanie Tantisira, MD

Melanie Tantisira, MD attended the University of Hawaii, John A. Burns, School of Medicine, where she was a member of the Alpha Omega Alpha medical honor society, and graduated at the top of her class in 1990. She completed her internship at the University of Hawaii, followed by her residency training in Ophthalmology at the University of Pittsburgh School of Medicine. Her hospital privileges are with the largest hospital in Hawaii, Queen’s Medical Center.
