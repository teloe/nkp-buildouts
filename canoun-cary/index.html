Deciding to undergo cosmetic surgery is a very important and personal decision It is essential that you choose the best surgeon for you. one that you trust. This is why you need to know about the training and experience of the surgeon you are considering.

This site provides information on Dr. Canoun, pre and postoperative photos, and descriptions of common aesthetic procedures.

Dr. Canoun has been practicing plastic surgery for over 20 years. He is a Harvard trained, double board certified plastic surgeon. He was previously director of Craniofacial surgery at the Medical College of Virginia.

Canoun Custom Facelift

Dr. Canoun's technique combines short scar with traditional facelift techniques. This produces significant but natural facial rejuvenation.
