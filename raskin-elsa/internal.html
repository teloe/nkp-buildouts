Why get a facelift?

As we age, there are many factors that can affect the elasticity and appearance of our skin such as sun exposure, stress, lifestyle, and genetics. This can be distressing to people who look in the mirror and no longer feel like they are looking at themselves. A facelift can be the perfect solution for individuals who are experiencing loose or sagging skin, wrinkles, or excess neck tissue.
