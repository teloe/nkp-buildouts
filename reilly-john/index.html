Meet Dr. Reilly

Personalized Care in Shelton, CT

Dr. Reilly has built a reputation for trusted, personalized care in Shelton, CT and the surrounding areas. His expertise in plastic surgery is a reason he was appointed as Chief, Section of Plastic Surgery at the Griffin Hospital.

Based on his years of experience, Dr. Reilly can help provide you with all the information needed to make an informed decision on what procedures will give you the best results. Dr. Reilly takes pride in helping transform people’s lives through improving their appearance. His true passion is knowing what it does for the person inside, allowing their dreams to come true and empowering them to feel better about themselves. The smile on the face of a patient after surgery says it all. Come discuss your goals regarding cosmetic surgical procedures with Dr. Reilly. You will learn more about them than you ever could have imagined!
