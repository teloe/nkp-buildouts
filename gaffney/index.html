BOTOX is a treatment that will temporarily improve the appearance of fine lines. Fine lines appear due to the facial muscles contracting repeatedly from frowning, squinting, and smiling. Wrinkles occur because of this repeated muscle action, and because of changes at the cellular level - namely a reduction in collagen production. Sun exposure and toxins also take a toll. With our Los Angeles BOTOX treatments, the muscle activity is temporarily reduced, giving a smoother, younger look to your features.
