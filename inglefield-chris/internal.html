  • Hairline Lowering

Hairline lowering surgery, also known as forehead lowering, forehead reduction, hairline advancement, hairline correction, and foreheadplasty, is used to lower the hairline, reduce the height of the forehead, and correct normal temple recession to create a more rounded hairline.

This facial plastic surgery procedure is commonly combined with brow lift surgery, or brow contouring surgery. Where significant hairline recession or male-pattern hair loss has occurred, hair transplantation may be necessary either on its own or in combination with hairline lowering surgery.

With over 20 years of experience in the field, Mr. Chris Inglefield and his team are committed to offering the most up to date and innovative treatments and procedures and the highest standard of care in a stress free and caring environment.
