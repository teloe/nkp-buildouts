Meet Dr. Anoush Hadaegh

Dr. Anoush Hadaegh has been a board certified Plastic surgeon for more than 15 years with vast experience in cosmetic and reconstructive surgery. He has been practicing on the North shore since 2010 and prior to that he practiced at Bay State Plastic surgery. His patients still travel more than 2 hours to be cared for by him. Dr. Hadaegh served for eight years as an assistant professor of Plastic Surgery at Tufts School of Medicine where he trained residents and medical students.

He was a frequent lecturer for his expertise in Breast Augementation and Breast Reconstruction, Body Contouring (especially after weight loss), Botox, and Fillers. Dr. Hadaegh attended medical school at Oregon Health University in Portland, Oregon and he completed his general surgery residency at Boston University Medical center.
