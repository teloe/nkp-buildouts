Research at Northwest Breast Center

Northwest Breast Center’s physicians influence breast reconstruction nationally through cutting-edge research. Our surgeons have published over 120 peer-reviewed manuscripts in the medical literature, the majority of which are centered on breast cancer, breast cancer reconstruction, and patient safety after surgical procedures.
