Dr. Cap Lesesne

Dr. Lesesne is a board certified plastic surgeon and is a active member of the American Society of Plastic Surgery (ASPS). He is the only American-trained plastic surgeon licensed to practice in both the United States and the United Kingdom, he is also board certified by the General Medical Council (GMC) in the UK.

Dr. Lesesne is skilled at aesthetic cosmetic procedures as well as highly complex, multi-layered facial plastic surgery that demands expert knowledge of all aspects of facial anatomy. After your first consultation, Dr. Lesesne will analyze photographs of you from 10, 20, or 30 years ago, to ensure the most natural and refined results possible.
