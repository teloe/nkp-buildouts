Meet Dr. Kathleen Albrecht

Dr. Kathleen Albrecht is a board-certified ophthalmologist with subspecialty training in Oculoplastic and Reconstructive surgery of the eyelids, orbit and face.

Aging, genetics and sun exposure can lead to drooping and bagginess of the eyes. However, these changes may also affect the ability of the lids to protect the eye, affect the vision and certainly the comfort of the eye with problems such as dry, irritated eyes or tearing eyes occurring. Medical conditions such as allergies, thyroid disease and skin cancer can also affect the eyelids.
