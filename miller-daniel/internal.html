Dental Implants

Dental Implants Portland

A dental implant, simply put, is an artificial tooth which mimics a natural root and crown. There are a variety of reasons people have implants placed. At Valley Dental Works in Portland, our goal is to provide our patients with excellent care from start to finish. We can provide you with dental implants that look natural and are fully functional.

THE BENEFITS OF DENTAL IMPLANTS

There are many reasons why someone may need or benefit from getting dental implants, including:

  • To replace congenitally missing teeth
  • To prevent bone loss
  • When only a root remains with no room to restore a crown
