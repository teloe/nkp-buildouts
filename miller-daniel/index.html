  • Meet Dr. Daniel Miller

    Dr. Miller has the unique opportunity to serve his hometown of Happy Valley, Oregon. He has been a part of this community for over 35 years, with a commitment and involvement to local athletics, small businesses, and schools. This involvement provides rewarding opportunities to care for the health and happiness of his patients.

    Dr. Miller is passionate about being able to offer the highest quality dentistry available. Each year, he attends extensive postdoctoral studies in clinical and cosmetic dentistry, enabling him to provide the latest advances in dental technology.
