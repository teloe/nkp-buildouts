The founding of DocbookKeeping

In 2015 we hired a CPA who was an expert in the health care industry to help navigate the business side of a struggling medical practice. When our medical practice began to realize millions of dollars in revenue that was previously missed by other companies/vendors, we knew we could help other healthcare professionals by creating DocBookkeeping. The average accounting practice, business analyst and billing company works in many different industries and does not know what specific financial advice to give Health Care Providers, especially if their other clients are in the retail industry or the manufacturing industry. By circumventing traditional channels, we put together a team of knowledgeable financial experts that can help with the creation and maintenance of a thriving healthcare practice.
