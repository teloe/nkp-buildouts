About Us

Since 1989, Western Surgical Care has been dedicated to keeping the Rocky Mountain region healthy and active. We provide compassionate and comprehensive surgical care for each and every patient, making individualized patient care our number one priority.

Our professionalism, approachability, and surgical expertise continues to attract patients from Denver and the entire Rocky Mountain region – many of our patients are actually referred by friends and family we’ve treated in the past. Our ultimate goal is to provide you with an outstanding surgical experience and compassionate care so you can recover quickly and get back to doing the things you love.
