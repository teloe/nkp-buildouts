Flawless Medspa

Flawless MedSpa is designed to correct cosmetic imperfections. Our medical professionals perform anti-aging and skin enhancement procedures in a relaxing spa environment. We have the expertise and technology to create the best experience for your laser hair removal, anti-aging and skin resurfacing procedures,fat reduction and injectables.

Our team specializes in minimally invasive and non-invasive medical aesthetic procedures. Your safety, health, and results are our TOP priority. We strive to deliver exemplary customer service in a relaxed and calming spa environment. Our team will spend time with you to define your aesthetic goals.

About our injectors

Lisa Allison RN and Mary Meagher RN both have extensive experience in the aesthetic arts, and have developed their skills and expertise in the use of medical grade products and injectable skincare. Both Lisa and Mary are Master Nurse Injectors and have beenextensively trained on advanced nonsurgical techniques.

They also have a Platinum Plus injector Account Status with Allergan, the maker of Botox®, Juvederm XC, Voluma XC, and Kybella which means they are ranked among the TOP 8% of users for volume in the United States. Lisa and Mary were selected by Allergan, to be among the first injectors of Kybella® due to their expertise with injectable aesthetic products.
