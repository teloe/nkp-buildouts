Facial Plastic Surgery

Atlanta and Alpharetta Facial Plastic Surgeon Dr. Philip Robb Jr.,M.D. performs his signature NO DRAIN facelift- all of Dr. Robb Jr., M.D.’s facelift are performed without the use of drains. No tubes dangling and pulling on your skin. No drains to remove after surgery! Dr. Robb Jr. can perform your facelift while awake, lightly sedated in the office, or in an accredited surgery center while you are comfortable and asleep.
