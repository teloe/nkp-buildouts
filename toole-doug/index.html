Doug Toole, DPM

Utah Foot and Ankle

Dr. Douglas A. Toole DPM is a male foot and ankle surgeon in Salt Lake City, UT with over 8 years of experience. Dr. Toole is affiliated with Flegal Clinic in Salt Lake City, as well as Salt Lake City VA Medical Center in Bountiful.

Dr. Toole graduated from Ohio College of Podiatric Medicine in 2012. He is licensed to practice by the state board in Utah (8718611).

Taylor G. Wright, DPM

Utah Foot and Ankle

Dr. Taylor G Wright was born and raised in Draper, Utah. He grew up in a family of 13 children – 10 boys and 3 girls – all from the same mother and father. He is very proud and grateful for having been raised in such an environment where attributes of hard work and service were not only expected, but were exemplified by his parents.

He grew up in a family where classical music was of prime emphasis and each child was expected to learn the piano as well as an additional musical instrument. Dr. Wright is a concert cellist and has played with many string quartets, orchestras, and surrounding symphonic groups.
