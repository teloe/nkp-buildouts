Advanced Sports Therapies

Sports-related foot and ankle injuries require careful evaluation and diagnosis by a podiatric surgeon like Dr. Taylor Wright in Salt Lake City. Foot and ankle injuries often result in torn ligaments, Achilles tendonitis, strained muscles, and in severe cases, broken bones and joint problems.

At Utah Foot and Ankle, our advanced sports therapies provide pain relief and improve performance after an injury. Dr. Wright and his team will get you back in action as quickly and safely as possible.

Dr. Wright is passionate about helping patients however he can, whether it means KT tape and athletic orthotics or a one-on-one running evaluation. Utah Foot and Ankle is the place where runners can go for expert care.

Running Evaluation in Salt Lake City

Whether you run for fun or for sport, you can benefit from a detailed running evaluation. The repetitive nature of running and the load on the joints from your hips down to your toes can result in overuse injuries affecting the hip, knee, ankle, and foot.

  • Musculoskeletal Exam
  • Biomechanical Analysis
  • Strength, Flexibility, and Functional Evaluation
  • Gait Assessment (Video Analysis)
