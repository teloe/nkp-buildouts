Cool Springs Plastic Surgery

Cool Springs Plastic Surgery is a team of medical professionals wholeheartedly devoted to performing the craft of plastic surgery and aesthetic services for the benefit of our patients.

We recognize that external appearance is valuable in our world, and having a heart that is confident, secure and full is the aim of our patients. At CSPS we use our skills to create, enhance and restore external features in pursuit of those goals. We perform these efforts as beautifully as possible for the glory of God and love of our patients. We love what we do!

Care and Comfort

4 Locations | On-Site Surgery Center | 3-story Building

Cool Springs Plastic Surgery is honored to serve our patients across 4 various locations in the surrounding Nashville area. Located out of our 3-story Brentwood office, we offer patients an excellent surgical experience with the utilization of our own state-of-the-art surgery center. With surgical staff boasting over 150 years of combined experience, our surgery center ensures a private, safe environment for each of our patients. Between our doctors and aesthetic providers, we perform over 2,500 surgical procedures and over 10,000 non-surgical treatments yearly. Through these immense resources, we are proud to offer convenience and ease to our patients throughout their entire aesthetic journey.
