Our providers will perform a thorough examination and answer any questions you may have regarding the CoolSculpting® procedure. CoolSculpting® is a safe, effective non-surgical body contouring treatment for men and women who struggle with stubborn fat on the thighs, abdomen, back or arms or have a double chin. Sometimes diet and exercise are not enough to reduce or eliminate isolated areas of fat. Now there is a safe, effective and non-surgical solution with CoolSculpting® fat reduction in Guilford, CT at Yolo Laser Center & Med Spa. CoolSculpting is clinically proven to remove up to 20% to 40% of fat cells from diet- and exercise-resistant areas of the body. By targeting and freezing the fat cells under the skin, FDA-cleared CoolSculpting at Yolo Laser Center allows your body to naturally eliminate fat withou damaging surrounding skin or muscle.
