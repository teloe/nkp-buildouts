Whitby Botox Cosmetic

Many of our specialized treatments involve the use of Botox injections, Dermal Fillers, and other injectables. Treatment plans are designed with individual client goals in mind. Our highly qualified medical team is there to make the best recommendations for optimal results.

Many clients inquire about Botox as it is a popular treatment used to prevent the signs of aging skin. As a neuromodulator, Botox is injected by our professional staff to temporarily help paralyze a part of a muscle, which causes the pull on the surface of the skin, therefore preventing the wrinkle. Over time, Botox can even shrink existing wrinkles for a more youthful look and smoother skin texture.

01

Lasting Results

You may begin to notice results within 24 to 48 hours, with results lasting up to four months for moderate to severe frown lines. Remember that results vary from patient to patient though, so your nurse will plan your next appointment based on your results and aesthetic goals.

02

Minor Discomfort

Some minor discomfort can be expected with any injection. People most often describe the sensation as minor pin pricks. Some mild swelling is usually visible at the site of injection, but dissipates within a few minutes. Some more sensitive skins may see the redness and bumps last up to 24 hours. The devices and needles used in cosmetic enhancements are often much smaller in size than an injection needle used for a vaccine. Because they are so small, they do not hurt as much as the larger ones.

03

Natural Expression

Once I start using Botox, what will happen to my face if I ever decide to stop? In Botox, the medication is only active for a maximum of 50 days. However, the effects from the medication may take four to six months before normal muscle expression is returned. It is a very gradual development and is often not noticed until full expression is returned. Ultimately, if you stop using Botox, your face and its expressions will only return to what they were functioning at before the injection was introduced.

04

Subtle Results

Will my face look overdone or unnatural? BOTOX Cosmetic is a technique-sensitive treatment. You can trust BOTOX Cosmetic to deliver subtle results when you are treated by someone who is licensed, trained and a medical expert in facial anatomy.
