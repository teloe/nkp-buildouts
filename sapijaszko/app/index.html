Mission Statement

Youthful Image was built and continues striving based on Dr. Marusz's vision: dedication, passion, and knowledge.

DR. MARIUSZ SAPIJASZKO

Dr. Mariusz Sapijaszko is a nationally and internationally trained Dermatologist, Cosmetic Surgery and Laser Surgery Expert. He is a Medical Director of the Western Canada Dermatology Institute and Youthful Image Clinic, a Non-Hospital Surgical Centers. Dr. Sapijaszko is a Clinical Associate Professor at the University of Alberta Division of Dermatology and is involved in training future dermatologists. He is a lecturer at national and international events presenting topics in the fields of skin cancer as well as cosmetic surgery.
